Understanding Gradient Descent

Gradient descent is an optimization algorithm used to minimize some function by iteratively moving in the direction of steepest descent as defined by the negative of the gradient. In machine learning, we use gradient descent to update the parameters of our model.

Conceptual Analogy

Imagine you are blindfolded in a rough terrain, and your objective is to reach the lowest altitude. You would feel the slope of the ground around you and step in the direction that goes down the fastest.
